# Research Index

## 1. Purpose
The Research Index establishes the framework for future benchmarks and studies. As a platform where "Knowledge Must Compound" (PRN-011) and "Data Is the Primary Asset" (PRN-014), we must continually research and measure our impact.

## 2. Research Pillars
- **Trust Metrics**: How effectively does transparency build trust? (Measuring dispute resolution times, user retention).
- **Guidance Efficacy**: How often does the Silent Advisor improve outcomes? Are humans empowered? (PRN-001).
- **System Performance**: Benchmarks on reliability ("The Platform Never Sleeps").

## 3. Future Benchmarks
- Define standard metrics for industrial transaction efficiency.
- Establish baselines for onboarding time and time-to-first-value.
- Create open (anonymized) datasets for industry research to foster the ecosystem.
