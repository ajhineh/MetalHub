# Audit & Trust Mechanisms

## 1. Introduction
Trust on MetalHub is designed into the workflow (PRN-004). Transparency builds trust (PRN-013), and our audit mechanisms are the technical manifestation of this philosophy.

## 2. Traceability (Every Decision Must Be Traceable)
- **Immutable Logs**: All critical transactions, decisions, and system state changes must be recorded in an immutable audit log.
- **Decision Continuity**: The context of decisions is saved alongside the actions, so future generations (or future audits) can understand the logic.

## 3. Verifiable Evidence
- **Evidence Over Claims (PRN-010)**: Audit trails provide the verifiable evidence required to resolve disputes and track performance.
- System actions (e.g., Silent Advisor recommendations) are also audited to ensure accountability and compounding knowledge.

## 4. Trust Engineering
- Dashboards surface audit data transparently to relevant stakeholders.
- "Trust Scores" or reliability metrics are calculated strictly based on audited historical data.
