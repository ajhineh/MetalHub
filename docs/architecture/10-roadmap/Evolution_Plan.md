# Platform Evolution Plan

## 1. Overview
The evolution of MetalHub follows a structured path, ensuring that knowledge compounds over time (PRN-011) and that every new phase creates more value for the ecosystem (The Network Value Principle - PRN-018).

## 2. Phase 1: MVP (Foundation & Trust)
- **Goal**: Establish the core workflow and prove "Evidence Over Claims".
- **Focus**: Basic matching, secure authentication, and foundational audit trails. 
- **Milestone**: First successful, traceable transactions executed on platform.

## 3. Phase 2: Beta (Guidance & Expansion)
- **Goal**: Introduce the "Silent Advisor" to provide adaptive guidance.
- **Focus**: Refining the data models, expanding integrations, and improving the feedback loops so that Knowledge Compounds.
- **Milestone**: Users report that platform guidance saves time and reduces risk.

## 4. Phase 3: Version 1.0 (The Industrial Standard)
- **Goal**: Achieve "The Platform Never Sleeps".
- **Focus**: High availability, comprehensive CI/CD, advanced analytics, and full regulatory compliance.
- **Milestone**: Wide industry adoption and recognition as the primary source of truth.

## 5. Future Horizons
- Deeper AI integration for predictive industrial trends.
- Automated compliance verification.
- Ecosystem-wide knowledge graphs.
