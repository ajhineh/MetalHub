# Decision Philosophy

Our approach to decision-making is rooted in the belief that uncertainty is the greatest enemy of industrial execution. To combat this, we adhere to the following philosophies:

1. **Evidence Over Claims**
Decisions must be based on verifiable data, past performance, and clear documentation, rather than marketing claims or unsupported promises.

2. **Every Decision Must Be Traceable**
The logic behind any decision must be recorded so that it is understandable to future generations. This ensures Decision Continuity.

3. **Knowledge Must Compound**
Every interaction on the platform should enrich the overall Industrial Memory. We do not just solve a problem once; we learn from it so that the next time, the decision is faster and safer.

4. **Guidance Must Be Adaptive**
The system should provide context-aware recommendations (Guidance) that empower humans to make the final call. The platform serves to augment human judgment, not to replace it.

5. **Transparency Builds Trust**
Trust cannot be manufactured; it must be designed into the workflow. By making the execution transparent and surfacing potential risks early, we align the interests of all participants.
