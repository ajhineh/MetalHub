# Purpose

The deeper philosophical purpose of MetalHub, its "Essence", is:

**To increase the quality of collective industrial judgment.**
*(ارتقای کیفیت قضاوت جمعی در صنعت)*

This essence transcends any specific technology, industry, or product feature. If the platform ever expanded beyond metals or custom manufacturing, its core purpose would remain exactly the same. MetalHub exists to ensure that human judgment is amplified, supported by evidence, and preserved for the future.
