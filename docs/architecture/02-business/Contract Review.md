# Contract Review

## Goal
To ensure commercial agreements accurately reflect validated technical requirements, agreed terms, and risk allocations, fostering a foundation of trust before execution begins. It aligns the contractual reality with the digital truth of the project.

## Inputs
- Finalized supplier proposals and tender evaluations
- Standard terms and conditions (Incoterms, payment terms)
- Project scope, milestones, and SLA definitions
- Risk registers generated during the evaluation phase

## Outputs
- Approved and digitally signed contract document
- Updated project risk register
- Agreed execution milestones and Work Packages
- Finalized Digital Case ready for execution

## Actors
- Legal Reviewer
- Procurement Manager
- Capability Provider (Supplier)
- Client

## Rules
- Contracts must strictly align with the validated digital case; no out-of-band modifications are permitted without a formal decision log.
- Contracts must define clear SLAs and tie them to verifiable evidence (e.g., QC reports for payment release).
- Ambiguities must be flagged by the system prior to approval to prevent Knowledge Debt.

## Success Metrics
- Contract turnaround and approval time
- Post-contract dispute rate
- Alignment score between initial requirements and final contract terms
- Number of automated risk flags resolved prior to signature
