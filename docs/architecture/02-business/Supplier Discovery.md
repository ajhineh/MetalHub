# Supplier Discovery

## Goal
To identify and recommend the most capable, trustworthy, and reliable providers for specific industrial requirements using a dynamic Trust Graph. This capability ensures that supplier matching is based on objective evidence rather than unverified claims, fostering a transparent industrial ecosystem.

## Inputs
- Initial project requirements and technical specifications
- Required manufacturing or engineering capabilities
- Geographical and logistical preferences
- Historical supplier performance data and Trust Scores
- Evidence from past projects within the Industrial Memory

## Outputs
- A ranked list of verified supplier profiles
- Capability match analysis
- Supplier-specific risk assessments
- Defendability checklist for selection

## Actors
- Procurement Manager
- Client
- AI Copilot (Trust Engine)

## Rules
- Supplier recommendations must be based on objective past performance and verifiable evidence, not arbitrary marketing claims.
- The system must prioritize suppliers with a proven track record of quality and adherence to SLAs in similar historical projects.
- Discovery results must include clear justifications to reduce decision friction.

## Success Metrics
- Time required to identify a qualified supplier
- Percentage of successful and accepted matches
- Supplier responsiveness and acceptance rate
- Long-term improvement in project success rates based on system recommendations
