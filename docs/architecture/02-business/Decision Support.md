# Decision Support

## Goal
To provide users with contextual evidence, historical data, and actionable guidance to reduce professional uncertainty and help them make defendable decisions. This capability is the core of the platform's Decision Intelligence.

## Inputs
- User's current project context and role
- Decision friction indicators (e.g., time spent on a page, repeated option toggling)
- Historical project data and precedents
- Active risk registers and capability constraints

## Outputs
- Contextual recommendations and progressive guidance
- Summaries of relevant evidence
- Risk warnings and defendability checklists
- Automated logging of the final decision and its justification

## Actors
- All decision-making users
- AI Copilot (Decision Friction Engine / Journey Intelligence Engine)

## Rules
- The system must guide but never force decisions, preserving human agency.
- All recommendations must be traceable to objective data within the Industrial Memory.
- System guidance must adapt dynamically based on the user's observed state of uncertainty.

## Success Metrics
- Speed of decision-making
- User confidence and satisfaction ratings
- Reduction in decision reversals or post-decision rework
- Frequency of users accepting system recommendations
