# Risk Detection

## Goal
To proactively identify potential issues in project feasibility, supplier reliability, or execution timelines before they materialize into failures. This capability leverages the Digital Thread to provide early warnings and safeguard project success.

## Inputs
- Real-time execution data and milestone progress
- Historical defect rates and NCR frequencies
- Supplier performance trends and current capacity
- Engineering validations and DFM (Design for Manufacturability) analyses

## Outputs
- Early warning alerts and notifications
- Mitigation recommendations and alternative options
- Automatically updated risk registers
- Contextual guidance during decision gates

## Actors
- Project Manager
- Client
- Procurement Manager
- AI Copilot

## Rules
- Risk alerts must be actionable and grounded in objective data, avoiding alarm fatigue.
- Detected risks must halt progression at predefined Decision Gates until formally addressed or accepted.
- Mitigation strategies must draw from validated lessons learned in the Industrial Memory.

## Success Metrics
- Number of risks mitigated proactively before causing delay or cost overrun
- Overall reduction in project delays
- Decrease in non-conformance reports (NCRs) and defects
- User responsiveness to risk alerts
