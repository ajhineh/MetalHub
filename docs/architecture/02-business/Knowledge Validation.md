# Knowledge Validation

## Goal
To verify the accuracy, relevance, and applicability of captured knowledge before it becomes an established part of the shared Industrial Memory ecosystem. This capability ensures the integrity and reliability of the platform's intelligence.

## Inputs
- Raw captured experiences and execution data
- Draft lessons learned and incident reports
- Project execution evidence (photos, documents, QA logs)
- Community or peer feedback

## Outputs
- Validated knowledge entries added to the Industrial Memory
- Updated architectural, operational, or engineering guidelines
- Refined AI models and guidance rules
- Knowledge debt reduction reports

## Actors
- Architecture Review Board
- Domain Experts / Quality Assurance
- System (AI Copilot)

## Rules
- Knowledge must be backed by objective evidence and clearly linked to specific project outcomes.
- Unverified or subjective claims cannot be promoted to global guidance without expert validation.
- Validated knowledge must align with established architectural principles.

## Success Metrics
- Accuracy and reliability of organizational memory
- Frequency and speed of knowledge validation cycles
- Trust ratings from users regarding system recommendations
- Reduction in repeated errors across the ecosystem
